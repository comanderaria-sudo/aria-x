## The Solution: ARIA-X

**Find → Fix → Recover**

ARIA-X automatically identifies revenue recovery opportunities and provides AI-powered recommendations for action.

### How It Works

1. **Find** - Scan emails, calendar, and invoices for opportunities
2. **Fix** - Get AI-powered recommendations for recovery actions
3. **Recover** - Execute actions and track revenue recovered

### What ARIA-X Detects

✓ **Unpaid invoices** - Overdue, due soon, high-value
✓ **Dormant leads** - Unread emails, no-reply conversations
✓ **Scheduling gaps** - Empty time for sales calls
✓ **Missed opportunities** - Calendar conflicts, scheduling inefficiencies

### Real Demo Results

**In 60 seconds, ARIA-X found:**
- 3 overdue invoices: **$8,200**
- 3 dormant leads: **$3,500**
- 2 scheduling conflicts: **$1,000** potential

**Total: $11,700 in recovery opportunities**
